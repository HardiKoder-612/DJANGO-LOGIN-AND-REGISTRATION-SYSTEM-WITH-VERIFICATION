The purpose of our Login and Registration System is to provide the users an easier way to enter a website using authorization. Only those users, who have an account with us, will be able to access the site. New users can register themselves and avail the facility for the same. Its aim is to enhance the existing manual system using Django. Since, its software-based access, there would be a lesser chance of the data being corrupted and therefore it would be more secure and reliable. It can also ease the burden of the record keeping person as now they can concentrate on their other tasks rather than spending their time on record keeping, which will ultimately lead to better resource utilization. The aim of our project is to automate the whole login and registration process, without the need of any human interference using softwares. There would also be no need to store the credentials anywhere, as it would be automatically stored, securely in the backend.
